DELTARUNE: CROSSCUT

A Post-Scratch DELTARUNE Modification

What happens when the story refuses to end... but breaks instead?

♥ The Story

Susie wanted the story to stay the same forever. She got her wish... sort of.


In DELTARUNE: Crosscut, a botched universe reset has left the world eerily familiar yet fundamentally changed. Chara Dreemurr now stands where Kris once did — a mischievous 15-and-a-half-year-old with a pair scissors, a love of chocolate, and a talent for pushing everyone's buttons.


The pieces look the same, but they don't quite fit together anymore. And somewhere in the shadows lurks the Answer Of Fate...

♦ Features

  • Play as Chara Dreemurr instead of Kris
  • Completely rewritten dialogue reflecting Chara's sarcastic personality
  • New character titles from "Troublemaker" to "Nap Scout"
  • Enhanced Weird Route with darker edge and new spells like IceSickle
  • Post-Scratch lore tying back to Susie's reset
  • Custom sound effects and animations
  • New Light World gear fitting Chara's prankster personality
  • Dark World equipment reimagined as dangerous blades and artifacts
